What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a measure of a student’s overall academic performance across all subjects. It is commonly used in schools and universities to evaluate student performance.

What is the difference between GPA and CGPA?
GPA is calculated for a single semester, while CGPA is the overall average across multiple semesters.
