What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method where you work for 25 minutes (one Pomodoro) and then take a 5-minute break. After completing several sessions, you take a longer break. This helps improve concentration and efficiency.

How It Works

  • Set your work timer (usually 25 minutes)
  • Start working on your task
  • Take a short break (5 minutes) after each session
  • Repeat the cycle
  • Take a longer break after 4 sessions
